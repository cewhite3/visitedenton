#1 Edenton North Carolina Bed & Breakfast Inn on TripAdvisor

and Mulberry Hill on the Sound Event Venue

The Inner Banks Inn is a romantic bed and breakfast set on 2.75 acres in the famed Edenton NC Historic District. An easy stroll to the downtown and Edenton Bay waterfront, this Diamond Collection Inn is a top rated Bed and Breakfast and Dining/Event Experience in TripAdvisor’s NC Coast Region. The historic inn has twenty rooms and suites with modern conveniences and a choice of amazing breakfast options. From romantic weekends to curated events, relaxing moments await on the inn’s picturesque porches and throughout its charming courtyard and grounds.

Our Southern B&B Stay offers a full service breakfast experience with entree selections, with original heritage Carolina Plantation Grits, and locally sourced bacon and sausage. Guests booking the Southern B&B Stay are served breakfast in our charming converted carriage house restaurant, The Table at Inner Banks.  If you are leaving early in the morning, we are happy to provide a breakfast sandwich to go.

Would you rather “Stay Your Way” and opt for the continental breakfast? Start with a lower base rate, and add just the options you want. Either way, we are here to provide the perfect Edenton stay experience for you.

Winner of the OpenTable North Carolina Coast Best 10 “Most Romantic” and “Special Occasion” Restaurants, and TripAdvisor’s “North Carolina Coast Best 20” out of +3500 restaurants, we look forward to serving your group or hosting your special dining/event experience. Private chef dinners, catered special events and groups are welcome by reservation.

Have you found your soul mate and ready to say, “I do?” Our historic bed and breakfast is a perfect setting for elopements and smaller weddings. Host a larger group with an amazing water view at Mulberry Hill on the Sound. Our stunning sound front wedding and special event venue located at Links at Mulberry Hill pairs our Diamond Collection™ lodging with award winning cuisine and professional event staff for a full service Coastal Carolina Wedding Experience. Contact us today to begin planning your Happily Ever Edenton™ Destination Wedding.

Whether you are joining us for a quiet romantic getaway in North Carolina or celebrating a special event, rest assured Inner Banks Inn will make it an experience to remember. We look forward to having you join us at our beautiful bed and breakfast in Edenton, NC, “Where Southern Hospitality Meets Historic Charm”.
