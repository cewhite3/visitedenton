About Edenton

 

Looking for seaside getaway? Visit Edenton NC located in Chowan Country on the Albemarle Sound. This small town is built on community and friendship with a group welcoming locals. North Carolina’s beautifully preserved first Colonial capital has been described as the prettiest small town in the South. Edenton visitors enjoy the most intact Colonial courthouse in America (circa 1767), the 1886 Roanoke River Lighthouse, bay cruises, historic trolley tours, paddling on Edenton Bay, free boat slips & a charming downtown near the waterfront.

What Edenton Offers

Activities

Downtown Edenton offers many local businesses and shops for visitors. Walking the strip you will find over 20 local businesses to shop from.

 

Dining

Check out Edenton's finest oysters from the Edenton Bay Oyster Bar while enjoying waterfront views. Guests can choose from a variety of food options sampling the sea.

Historic Sites

Edenton offers many activies to showcase the town's history. There is the Taylor Theatre, Ronoake River Lighthouse, and the Chowan County Court House just to name a few.

Where To Stay

Inner Banks Inn

The Inner Banks Inn is a romantic bed and breakfast set on 2.75 acres in the famed Edenton NC Historic District. An easy stroll to the downtown and Edenton Bay waterfront, this Diamond Collection Inn is a top rated Bed and Breakfast and Dining/Event Experience in TripAdvisor’s NC Coast Region. The historic inn has twenty rooms and suites with modern conveniences and a choice of amazing breakfast options. From romantic weekends to curated events, relaxing moments await on the inn’s picturesque porches and throughout its charming courtyard and grounds.

 

Captain's Quarters Inn

Located in Edenton’s Historic District, Captain’s Quarters Inn is a 8-room Bed and Breakfast. Each room has a private bath, cable TV, Wi-Fi, off street parking and many more amenities to make your stay as comfortable as possible. Whether this is your first visit, or you have been a guest many times, we want your experience to be excellent! Our staff is available to help with any questions or concerns.
